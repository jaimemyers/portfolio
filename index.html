About

Hi, my name is Jaime. I'm a librarian by day and dabble in web design by night. It's been a roundabout journey getting here. I collected degrees like they were Happy Meal toys for a while. If anyone cares, I majored in History, English, and International Studies—French, then went and got a niche M.A. in Medieval Studies.
