About

Hi, my name is Jaime. I'm a librarian by day and dabble in web design by night. It's been a roundabout journey getting here. I collected degrees like they were Happy Meal toys for a while. If anyone cares, I majored in History, English, and International Studies—French, then went and got a niche M.A. in Medieval Studies. Turns out, that doesn't pay well, so I topped it all off with a M.S. in Information Sciences. *Cough* nerd *cough*.

For the two of you still reading, I'm on the hunt for a job. Libraries, IT, web design — I've got the skills if you've got the need.
